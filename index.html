ABOUT US

Panditji ForU is a community of pandits who are working to bring a better source of income for our people. In this period of time, the purohit community is facing a lot of trouble, and thus to bridge the gap we can up with the solution of virtual poojan.

VISION

To ease our poojan with the use of technology and to place our Hindu culture in the heart of all of us.

MISSION

To bring the Pandit community together and to bring technology into our poojan.

GOAL

To implement the use of technology in our traditional poojan.
